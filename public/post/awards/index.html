Awards

ACM SIGSOFT is responsible, both directly or by encouraging organizers of SIGSOFT-sponsored conferences to designate their recipients, for several different awards.

SIGSOFT Awards

  • The Distinguished Service Award

  • The Outstanding Research Award

  • The Influential Educator Award

  • The Impact Paper Award

  • The Outstanding Doctoral Dissertation Award

  • The Early Career Researcher Award

  • The Frank Anger Memorial Award

Note: These awards are not given posthumously.

Conference Awards.

  • The ICSE Most Influential Paper Award

  • The ESEC/FSE Test of Time Award

  • The ASE Most Influential Paper Award

  • The ISSTA Impact and Retrospective Awards

  • The Distinguished Paper Award

Note: The list is not exhaustive.
